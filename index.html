Captive 101

What is Captive Insurance?

A specialized form of self-insurance that empowers businesses to take a proactive approach to managing risk.

Specialized Self-Insurance

Captive insurance companies are owned and controlled by the organizations they insure, providing a tailored approach to risk management.

Greater Control

More control over insurance policies, coverage terms, and risk management strategies than traditional commercial insurance.

Customized Solutions

Design coverage that specifically addresses your unique risk exposures without paying for unnecessary coverage.

Key Benefits

Why Now?

The commercial insurance market is shifting. Here's why businesses are turning to captive solutions.

Rising Premiums

U.S. commercial lines premiums increased ~3.8% year-over-year in Q2 2025, with excess/umbrella liability seeing sharper increases.

Market Volatility

Carriers tighten underwriting, raise deductibles, and demand more stringent terms for emerging risks.

Rising Costs

Inflation, rising materials and labor costs, and increased catastrophe exposure drive up claim costs.

Coverage Gaps

Coverage may be constrained or unavailable, especially for unique or hard-to-insure exposures.
